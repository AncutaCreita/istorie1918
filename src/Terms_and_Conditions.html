Introduction

Romania 1918 application is an interactive digital encyclopedia usable on any medium (phone, tablet, laptop, desktop – regardless of the operating system) designed to present to the general public, to both children and adults, moments in the history of Romanians, in a modern and novel way. It offers photo galleries put together from private and public collections, animated images, video clips, 3D objects, dynamic maps, epoch documents, interactive games adapted to facilitate access to information.

The application is provided in Romanian and English by SIVECO Romania S.A., free of charge, and is intended exclusively for educational purposes.

Using the application

The application provides information about the history of Romanians from 1914-1922 in an easily understandable way, using several types of interactive apps.

When you open the application, you will be prompted to choose the language in which the content will be presented and accept the terms and conditions in order to view and play the interactive games.
